# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Report suspected vulnerabilities privately to **security@promty.org**. Include the affected URL or component, reproduction steps, expected impact, and supporting evidence. Do not include credentials or unnecessary personal information in the initial message and do not open a public GitHub issue.

We aim to acknowledge a report within three business days, validate and prioritise it, and coordinate disclosure after a fix when practical.

## Safe research guidelines

- Test only accounts and data you control or are explicitly authorised to use.
- Avoid destructive actions, privacy violations, social engineering, denial of service, and high-volume automated scanning.
- Stop testing and report promptly if you encounter user data or gain unintended access.
- Give us a reasonable opportunity to investigate and remediate before public disclosure.

Good-faith research following these guidelines will not be treated as abuse. This is not a bug-bounty programme and payment is not promised.

For compromised accounts, privacy requests, abuse, or community content reports, use Promty Support instead.
