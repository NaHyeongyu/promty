# Promty Acceptable Use Policy

Effective: 21 July 2026

Promty supports legitimate professional and business software work. Users are responsible for the people, data, repositories, and systems affected by their use.

Users must not use Promty for:

- unlawful, fraudulent, deceptive, harassing, hateful, exploitative, or sexually abusive activity;
- malware, credential theft, unauthorised access, service disruption, security-control bypass, or harmful automated activity;
- submitting secrets, personal information, or confidential material without authorisation;
- infringing copyright, trade marks, privacy, publicity, or other rights;
- publishing another person's prompts, code, or identity without permission;
- relying on generated output without appropriate human review where an error could cause harm; or
- evading limits, suspensions, moderation, or account controls.

Only reviewed and authorised content may be published to community features. Suspected violations should be reported through Promty Support with the content URL, category, and reason.

Promty may remove or limit content, revoke tokens, suspend accounts, preserve relevant evidence, or report conduct where reasonably necessary. Enforcement considers severity, context, repetition, and risk, and provides a review path where practical.
